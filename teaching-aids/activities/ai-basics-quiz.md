# AI Basics Quiz — Session 1

**Format:** Kahoot-style (project questions, students answer on devices or raise hands)
**Questions:** 10 | **Time:** 10-15 minutes | **Difficulty:** Easy (no prior knowledge needed)

---

## Questions

### Q1: What does AI stand for?
A) Automated Intelligence  
B) **Artificial Intelligence** ✅  
C) Augmented Input  
D) Automatic Integration

### Q2: Which of these uses AI?
A) A calculator  
B) A light switch  
C) **Netflix recommendations** ✅  
D) A bicycle

### Q3: What type of AI is ChatGPT?
A) General AI  
B) Superintelligence  
C) **Narrow AI** ✅  
D) None of the above

### Q4: True or False: AI systems can think and feel like humans.
A) True  
B) **False** ✅  

*Explanation: AI mimics human-like responses but does not have consciousness,
emotions, or genuine understanding.*

### Q5: What does LLM stand for?
A) Large Logic Machine  
B) **Large Language Model** ✅  
C) Linear Learning Method  
D) Low-Level Memory

### Q6: How does an LLM like ChatGPT work?
A) It searches Google for answers  
B) **It predicts the next most likely word based on training data** ✅  
C) It thinks like a human brain  
D) It calls a human for help

### Q7: Which is an example of Narrow AI?
A) **Google Maps predicting traffic** ✅  
B) A robot that can do any job  
C) A computer smarter than all humans  
D) A toaster

### Q8: What is a hallucination in AI?
A) When AI sees ghosts  
B) **When AI makes up false information confidently** ✅  
C) When AI dreams  
D) When AI goes to sleep

### Q9: True or False: AI is a new technology invented in 2022.
A) True  
B) **False** ✅  

*Explanation: AI research began in the 1950s. What's new is the scale of
modern LLMs and their availability to the public.*

### Q10: Which career might involve working with AI?
A) Data scientist  
B) UX designer  
C) AI ethicist  
D) **All of the above** ✅
