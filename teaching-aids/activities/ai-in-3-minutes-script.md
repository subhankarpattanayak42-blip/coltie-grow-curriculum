# AI in 3 Minutes — Video Script & Storyboard

**Type:** Animated explainer video (whiteboard / motion graphics style)
**Duration:** 180 seconds | **Used in:** Session 1
**Tools:** Canva AI Video, Animaker, or screen-recorded slide animation

---

## Storyboard

| Time | Visual | Audio (Voiceover) |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| **0:00-0:15** | Title card: "AI in 3 Minutes" with brain/robot icon. Fast zoom in. | "You use AI dozens of times a day. But what is it, really? Let's find out in 3 minutes." |
| **0:15-0:35** | Split screen: Left = human brain, Right = computer chip. Both have patterns flowing. | "AI stands for Artificial Intelligence. It's when computers do tasks that normally require human intelligence — like understanding language, recognizing images, or making decisions." |
| **0:35-0:55** | 3 icons appear: Netflix logo, Google Maps logo, ChatGPT logo. Each pulses as mentioned. | "Every time Netflix suggests a show, Google Maps finds your route, or ChatGPT answers a question — that's AI at work. It's already everywhere." |
| **0:55-1:20** | Visual of a river → water being poured into a funnel → patterns coming out the other end. | "Think of AI like this: You give it millions of examples (data), it looks for patterns (training), and then it uses those patterns to answer new questions (prediction). That's it. No magic." |
| **1:20-1:45** | Car analogy animation: GPS showing one route (Narrow AI) → Human driver (General AI) → Alien driver (Superintelligence). | "There are three levels. Narrow AI does one thing really well — like a GPS. General AI could do anything a human can — it doesn't exist yet. Superintelligence would be smarter than all humans combined — we're not there yet." |
| **1:45-2:10** | Warning sign icon. Then a checklist with checkmarks and X marks. | "But AI has problems. It makes things up (hallucinations). It can be biased. It doesn't actually understand — it just predicts. Use it as a tool, not a replacement for your own thinking." |
| **2:10-2:35** | CUT to speaker on screen OR animated avatar talking to camera. | "Here's the key insight: AI won't take your job. A person who knows how to use AI will. That's why you're in this program — to become that person." |
| **2:35-3:00** | End card: COLTIE GROW logo + "Session 2: Prompt Engineering" text. Fade out. | "In our next session, we'll learn how to talk to AI effectively using prompt engineering. See you there!" |

---

## Production Notes

**Style:** Whiteboard animation or motion graphics with clean icons.
**Audio:** Record voiceover first, then sync visuals. Use a warm, conversational tone — not lecturing.
**Tools:**
- Canva AI Video (text-to-video)
- OBS Studio (screen recording)
- Audacity (audio recording, free)
- ElevenLabs (AI voiceover, optional)

**Length check:** Read the script aloud. It should land between 170-190 seconds at natural speaking pace.

**Assets needed:**
- Brain icon
- Computer chip icon
- Netflix/Google Maps/ChatGPT logos (simple versions)
- Funnel/diagram animation
- Car analogy graphics
- Warning sign icon
- COLTIE GROW end card
