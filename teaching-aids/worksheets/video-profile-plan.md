## Recording Checklist

- [ ] Natural light facing me, not behind me
- [ ] Camera at eye level (stack books under laptop if needed)
- [ ] Background is clean and uncluttered
- [ ] I'm looking at the camera lens, not the screen
- [ ] I practiced 3 times without recording
- [ ] My best take was #1 or #2 (first takes are most authentic)
- [ ] No reading — I'm using bullet points off-screen
- [ ] I'm dressed for a college interview
- [ ] Background noise is minimized
- [ ] I'm smiling and speaking naturally
