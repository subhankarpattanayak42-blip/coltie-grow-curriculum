# Session 8: Final Presentations & Graduation

**Duration:** 2 hours | **Format:** Live (with parents invited) | **Instructors:** Subhankar & Anuj

---

## Session Objectives

By the end of this session, students will be able to:
1. Deliver a 5-minute presentation of their COLTIE GROW journey
2. Share their portfolio with peers, instructors, and parents
3. Articulate their next steps in the college admissions process
4. Graduate with a complete Admissions Tech Stack

---

## Session Flow

| Time | Segment | Activity | Lead |
|------|---------|----------|------|
| 0:00–0:10 | **Opening Remarks** | Welcome parents, recap the 4-week journey | Subhankar |
| 0:10–1:10 | **Student Presentations** | 5 min each × 10-12 students | All |
| 1:10–1:20 | **Instructor Reflections** | Highlights, growth moments | Both |
| 1:20–1:30 | **Guest Speaker Closing** | Final words from industry guest | Guest |
| 1:30–1:40 | **Parent Q&A** | Post-program support, next steps | Both |
| 1:40–1:50 | **Certificate Ceremony** | Digital certificates, COLTIE profile URLs | Both |
| 1:50–2:00 | **Graduation Close** | Group photo, what's next for COLTIE alumni | Subhankar |

## Content Notes

### Evaluation Rubric

| Criterion | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Developing (2) | Needs Work (1) |
|-----------|---------------|----------|----------------|----------------|
| **Clarity** | Clear structure, easy to follow | Mostly clear | Some confusing parts | Hard to follow |
| **Personal Insight** | Deep reflection, specific examples | Good reflection | Generic statements | No personal insight |
| **AI Literacy** | Shows genuine understanding | Good grasp | Basic awareness | Confused |
| **Portfolio Quality** | All deliverables complete + polished | Complete | Partial | Incomplete |
| **Delivery** | Confident, engaging, good pacing | Good | Nervous but OK | Unprepared |

### Post-Program Next Steps

**Immediate (30 days):** Complete essays, finalize shortlist, register for standardized tests, build extracurricular profiles.

**Medium (3-6 months):** Begin applications, request recommendation letters, visit campuses, apply for scholarships.

**Long (6-12 months):** Submit applications, prepare for interviews, explore financial aid, connect with current students.
