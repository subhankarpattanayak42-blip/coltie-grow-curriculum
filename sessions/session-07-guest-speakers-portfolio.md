# Session 7: Guest Speakers & Portfolio Review

**Duration:** 90 minutes | **Format:** Live | **Instructors:** Subhankar & Anuj (+ Guest Speakers)

---

## Session Objectives

By the end of this session, students will be able to:
1. Hear from AI industry leaders about real-world applications
2. Understand career paths in AI from professionals
3. Get feedback on their portfolio from experts
4. Refine their final presentation plan

---

| Time | Segment | Activity | Lead |

## Content Notes

### Guest Speaker Briefing Questions (share with guest in advance)

1. What does your day-to-day work in AI actually look like?
2. What skill do you see that students today aren't building?
3. How has AI changed your field in the last 2 years?
4. What advice would you give your 16-year-old self?
5. What's one AI development you're most excited about?

### Portfolio Review Criteria

When reviewing student work, evaluate on:
- **Authenticity:** Does it sound/feel like a real student?
- **Specificity:** Are there concrete details or just general statements?
- **Growth:** Can you see learning and reflection?
- **AI Use:** Has AI been used as a tool or a crutch?

## Homework

1. **Final Presentation (2 hours over the week):** Prepare your 5-minute presentation.

**Structure:**
- 1 min: Who you were before COLTIE GROW
- 2 min: What you built (personal statement, shortlist, video)
- 1 min: What you learned about AI and yourself
- 1 min: What's next (next steps in your college journey)

**Format:** Slides (optional) + live talk. Can use Canva AI, Gamma, or any tool.

2. **Portfolio Final Polish:**
   - Personal statement: final read-through
   - Shortlist: double-check deadlines and requirements
   - Video: final cut uploaded to COLTIE
   - COLTIE profile: fully complete

3. **Test Your Tech:** Ensure screen sharing, camera, and microphone work for Final Day.
