# Session 3: Career Identity — Who Am I?

**Duration:** 90 minutes | **Format:** Live | **Instructors:** Subhankar & Anuj

---

## Session Objectives

By the end of this session, students will be able to:
1. Identify their Holland Code (RIASEC) personality type
2. Name their top 5 work values
3. Connect their interests to real career clusters
4. List 3 careers they'd never considered that align with their profile

## Content Notes

### Holland Code (RIASEC) Framework

| Code | Type | Traits | Example Careers |
|------|------|--------|----------------|
| **R** | Realistic | Hands-on, practical, mechanical | Engineer, pilot, surgeon, architect |
| **I** | Investigative | Curious, analytical, scientific | Data scientist, researcher, doctor |
| **A** | Artistic | Creative, expressive, innovative | Designer, writer, musician, filmmaker |
| **S** | Social | Helping, teaching, counseling | Teacher, psychologist, nurse, social worker |
| **E** | Enterprising | Leading, persuading, risk-taking | Entrepreneur, manager, lawyer, sales |
| **C** | Conventional | Organized, structured, detail-oriented | Accountant, banker, administrator, analyst |

**Teaching tip:** Most people are a combination of 2-3 types. Your 3-letter code (e.g., "SIA" or "ECR") is more useful than any single letter.

### Work Values Exercise

Present 10 work values. Students choose their top 5:

1. **Income** — earning a high salary
2. **Stability** — job security, predictable income
3. **Creativity** — opportunity to innovate and express
4. **Impact** — making a difference in the world
5. **Independence** — control over your schedule and work
6. **Collaboration** — working with a team
7. **Prestige** — respect and recognition
8. **Growth** — opportunities to learn and advance
9. **Location** — where you live and work
10. **Balance** — time for family, hobbies, rest

**AI Prompt for Career Exploration:**
> "I'm a 10th grader with Holland Code [X,Y,Z]. My top 3 work values are [value1], [value2], [value3]. Suggest 5 careers I might not have considered that match this profile. For each, tell me: what they do, average salary range in India and US, required education, and why they fit my profile."

## Activities

1. **RIASEC Self-Scoring:** Paper-based or digital assessment (15 min)
2. **Value Sorting:** Drag-and-drop ranked choice (10 min)
3. **AI Career Brainstorm:** Use ChatGPT to explore 5 careers matching their code

---

## Homework

1. **Career Research (45 min):** Pick 3 careers from the AI suggestions. Research:
   - Day in the life
   - Colleges that offer related programs
   - Salary range
   - How AI is changing this field

2. **Reflection:** "Before today, I thought I wanted to be [X]. Now I'm also curious about [Y] because..."

3. **Bring to next session:** A list of 5-10 universities you've heard of and are curious about
