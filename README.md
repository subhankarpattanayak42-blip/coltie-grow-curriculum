# COLTIE GROW — AI Literacy & College Admissions Bootcamp

**4-Week Live Online Program | Classes 9–12 | No Coding Experience Required**

[![Coltie](https://img.shields.io/badge/Coltie-GROW-blue)](https://www.coltie.com/grow)

## Overview

COLTIE GROW is an application-gated, 4-week experiential AI bootcamp for high school students (Grades 9–12) preparing for global university admissions. Students build genuine AI literacy, discover their career passions through data-backed frameworks, and graduate with a complete Admissions Tech Stack.

**Instructors:** Subhankar Pattanayak & Anuj Sharma

### Student Deliverables

By the end of the program, each student will have:
- ✅ Personal statement draft
- ✅ University shortlist (8-factor rubric)
- ✅ 60-second video profile
- ✅ Permanent public COLTIE profile URL
- ✅ AI literacy certification

## Curriculum Architecture

The program is organized into **4 modules** across **8 live sessions**:

| Module | Sessions | Theme |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Module 1: AI Foundations** | Session 1–2 | What AI is, how LLMs work, prompt engineering, AI ethics |
| **Module 2: Self Discovery** | Session 3–4 | Career identity (Holland Code, Work Values), university research |
| **Module 3: Portfolio Building** | Session 5–6 | Personal statement crafting, no-code AI toolkit, COLTIE profile |
| **Module 4: Launch** | Session 7–8 | Guest speakers, final presentations, action plan |

## Teaching Philosophy

1. **AI as thinking partner, not shortcut.** Students learn to use AI to amplify their own thinking, not replace it.
2. **Identity-first.** Career and university choices flow from self-understanding, not parental pressure or peer influence.
3. **Portfolio over test scores.** The program prioritizes authentic outputs (personal statement, video profile) over standardized metrics.
4. **No code required.** AI literacy is taught through concepts, prompting, and no-code tools — not programming.
5. **Global perspective.** University research covers India, US, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and Europe.
