Session 7: Guest Speakers & Portfolio Review

Duration: 90 minutes | Format: Live | Instructors: Subhankar & Anuj (+ Guest Speakers)

Session Objectives

By the end of this session, students will be able to: 1. Hear from AI industry leaders about real-world applications 2. Understand career paths in AI from professionals 3. Get feedback on their portfolio from experts 4. Refine their final presentation plan

Session Flow

Time Segment Activity Lead
0:00–0:10 Week Recap Key learnings, tool wins, video profile sharing Anuj
0:10–0:40 Guest Keynote AI industry leader presentation (NVIDIA / Capital One) Guest
0:40–0:55 Student Q&A with Guest Pre-submitted + live questions Guest + Both
0:55–1:10 Portfolio Walkthrough Students share screens — personal statement, shortlist, video Both
1:10–1:20 Guest Feedback Industry perspective on student work Guest
1:20–1:30 Final Presentation Prep Structure, timing, slide expectations Subhankar

Content Notes

Guest Speaker Briefing Questions (share with guest in advance)

  1. What does your day-to-day work in AI actually look like?
  2. What skill do you see that students today aren’t building?
  3. How has AI changed your field in the last 2 years?
  4. What advice would you give your 16-year-old self?
  5. What’s one AI development you’re most excited about?

Portfolio Review Criteria

When reviewing student work, evaluate on: - Authenticity: Does it sound/feel like a real student? - Specificity: Are there concrete details or just general statements? - Growth: Can you see learning and reflection? - AI Use: Has AI been used as a tool or a crutch?

Activities

  1. Guest Q&A — Live moderated session
  2. Portfolio Screenshare — 3-4 volunteers show their work
  3. Presentation Sign-Ups — Students commit to their final topic

Homework

  1. Final Presentation (2 hours over the week): Prepare your 5-minute presentation.

Structure: - 1 min: Who you were before COLTIE GROW - 2 min: What you built (personal statement, shortlist, video) - 1 min: What you learned about AI and yourself - 1 min: What’s next (next steps in your college journey)

Format: Slides (optional) + live talk. Can use Canva AI, Gamma, or any tool.

  1. Portfolio Final Polish:
  2. Personal statement: final read-through
  3. Shortlist: double-check deadlines and requirements
  4. Video: final cut uploaded to COLTIE

  5. COLTIE profile: fully complete

  6. Test Your Tech: Ensure screen sharing, camera, and microphone work for Final Day.
