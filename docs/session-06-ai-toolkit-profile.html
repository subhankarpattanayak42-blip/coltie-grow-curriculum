Session 6: No-Code AI Toolkit & COLTIE Profile

Duration: 90 minutes | Format: Live | Instructors: Subhankar & Anuj

Session Objectives

By the end of this session, students will be able to: 1. Use 3 no-code AI tools for productivity and creativity 2. Understand how AI can amplify their application process 3. Complete their COLTIE profile with career info, shortlist, and essay draft 4. Record or plan their 60-second video profile

Session Flow

Time Segment Activity Lead
0:00–0:10 Essay Draft Review Quick check-in — everyone shares one sentence they’re proud of Anuj
0:10–0:25 No-Code AI Tools Tour Canva AI, Perplexity, NotebookLM, Gamma, Claude — what each does best Subhankar
0:25–0:40 Tool Playtime Students try 2 tools in breakout rooms Both
0:40–0:55 60-Second Video Profile Best practices, examples, planning template Subhankar
0:55–1:10 COLTIE Profile Polish Complete profiles: add shortlist, essay draft, interests, career code Anuj
1:10–1:20 Final Presentation Prep What they’ll present on July 11 Both
1:20–1:30 Q&A + Homework — Both

Content Notes

No-Code AI Tools Overview

Tool Best For How Students Use It
Canva AI Visual design Create presentation slides, social media graphics, video thumbnails
Perplexity Research Deep university research with citations, fact-checking
NotebookLM Document analysis Upload application requirements, get summaries and answers
Gamma Presentation Generate polished slide decks from prompts
Claude/ChatGPT Writing partner Essay feedback, brainstorming, editing suggestions
Otter.ai Transcription Record and transcribe mock interviews, reflections

60-Second Video Profile Structure

Second Content Tips
0–10 Hook Your name + one surprising thing about you
10–25 Who you are Your interests, Holland Code, what drives you
25–45 What you want Career interests, type of university you’re looking for
45–55 Why COLTIE GROW What you learned, what you built
55–60 Close One memorable line + your COLTIE profile URL

Recording tips: - Natural light facing you, not behind you - Look at the camera lens, not the screen - Speak like you’re talking to a friend - No script reading — use bullet points off-screen - Wear what you’d wear to a college interview

Activities

  1. Tool Speed Dating: 5 min per tool, rotate through 3 stations
  2. Video Script Drafting: Fill out the 60-second template
  3. Profile Complete: Finalize COLTIE profile with all program outputs

Homework

  1. Video Record (45 min): Record your 60-second video profile.
  2. Max 3 takes. First take is usually the most authentic.

  3. Upload to COLTIE profile.

  4. Tool Exploration: Pick one no-code AI tool you haven’t used before. Try it for 20 minutes. Note one thing it did well and one thing it couldn’t do.

  5. Presentation Topic: Think about what you want to present on Final Day (July 11). Options:

  6. Your personal statement journey
  7. Your university shortlist and why
  8. What you learned about yourself through career exploration
  9. A demo of how you use AI responsibly
